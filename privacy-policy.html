Privacy Policy for AmpWave

Last updated: December 16, 2024

1. Introduction

AmpWave ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

AmpWave does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. All data processing happens locally on your device.

3. Cloud Storage Access

AmpWave integrates with third-party cloud storage services to allow you to import your music files:

  • Google Drive: We request read-only access to files you explicitly select. We do not access any files without your permission.
  • Dropbox: We request read-only access to files you explicitly select. We do not access any files without your permission.
  • Apple Music/iTunes: We access your purchased music library only when you grant permission.

We only access files you explicitly choose to import. Your cloud storage credentials and data remain with the respective service providers.

4. Data Storage

Music files you import are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or transmit your music files to any external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

AmpWave uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For authentication to access Google Drive
  • Dropbox SDK: For authentication to access Dropbox
  • Apple MediaPlayer: For accessing your iTunes/Apple Music library

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Dropbox Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

6. Analytics and Tracking

We do not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services. We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or any other telemetry.

7. Children's Privacy

AmpWave does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

8. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, your data security depends on your device's security settings. We recommend using device passcodes and encryption.

9. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can revoke cloud storage access at any time through your device settings
  • You can delete imported music files from the app at any time
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
